About Jinrui no Shōri

Jinrui no Shōri (Victory of Humanity in Japanese) is an exciting game inspired by the Attack on Titan (SNK) manga. Developed by a solo developer in his spare time, this fan game brings the bloody world of the titans to life.

Currently in beta, Jinrui no Shōri offers an incredible gameplay experience in which players use three-dimensional equipment to take down massive titans. Although the game is still in development with some bugs, simple gameplay and terrible UI, it promises an immersive and challenging adventure for fans of the genre.

Current Features

Three-Dimensional Equipment

Navigate the world with custom-made 3D maneuvering gear, adding a layer of complexity to titan slaying.

Titan Combat

Face off against towering titans in intense battles, testing your skills and strategy.

Immersive World

Explore a world inspired by the Attack on Titan universe, brought to life with custom graphics and audio. (For the moment there is only one map inspired by the District of Trost.)

Development Roadmap

  • Additional maps for diverse gameplay environments.
  • New and improved 3D models.
  • Enhanced three-dimensional equipment mechanics.
  • Improved user interface and menu system.
  • Virtual Reality (VR) version for Oculus Quest.
  • Enhanced soundtrack and audio effects.
  • Potential multiplayer mode.
  • Comprehensive bug fixes and optimizations.
